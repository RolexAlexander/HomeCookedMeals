Vege Tasteful Desert

A nutritious and indulgent granola bar loaded with premium chocolate chips

$599
★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Indulge in the perfect balance of nutrition and decadence with our signature Vege Tasteful Desert, a premium granola bar that satisfies your sweet tooth while nourishing your body. Crafted with whole, plant-based ingredients, this delightful treat delivers both flavor and sustenance in every bite.


Each bar is thoughtfully created using a blend of rolled oats, crunchy nuts, and seeds that provide essential proteins and healthy fats. We fold in generous amounts of premium dark chocolate chips that melt beautifully on your palate, creating a rich contrast to the hearty granola base. A touch of organic honey and maple syrup binds everything together while adding a natural sweetness that complements the chocolate perfectly.


Our Vege Tasteful Desert is perfect as an on-the-go breakfast, a pre-workout energizer, or a guilt-free afternoon treat. Each bar comes individually wrapped to maintain freshness and make it convenient to toss in your bag, desk drawer, or hiking pack. Whether you're fueling an active lifestyle or just looking for a wholesome snack that doesn't compromise on taste, this versatile granola bar delivers satisfaction in every bite.


Ingredients: Rolled oats, almonds, walnuts, pumpkin seeds, sunflower seeds, organic dark chocolate chips, honey, maple syrup, coconut oil, vanilla extract, sea salt, and cinnamon.
