GT Hallal Salmon Grilled

Premium Hallal-certified salmon flame-grilled to perfection

$1,299
★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Savor the exceptional taste of our GT Hallal Salmon Grilled, a signature dish crafted with premium, ethically-sourced Hallal salmon. Each fillet is carefully selected for its quality, texture, and rich flavor profile, then expertly seasoned with our special blend of herbs and spices.


Our chefs grill the salmon to absolute perfection over an open flame, creating a beautiful caramelized exterior while maintaining a tender, flaky interior that melts in your mouth. The natural oils of the salmon are preserved during the grilling process, enhancing its succulent texture and imparting a subtle smoky essence that complements the fish's natural flavors.


Served with a vibrant medley of seasonal vegetables and a light citrus glaze that brightens the dish without overwhelming the star of the plate – our perfect salmon fillet. Every bite offers a harmonious balance of flavors, making this dish both nourishing and indulgent.


Ingredients: Hallal-certified Atlantic salmon, organic herbs, olive oil, lemon, garlic, and our secret blend of spices.
