Gourmet Egg Spread

A delightful breakfast favorite made with farm-fresh eggs and premium ingredients

$899
★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Elevate your breakfast or brunch experience with our signature Gourmet Egg Spread, a velvety and flavorful creation that transforms the humble egg into a culinary masterpiece. Made with organic, free-range eggs from local farms, our spread is rich in texture and abundant in taste.


Each batch is carefully prepared by our skilled chefs who fold in finely chopped chives, crisp bacon bits, creamy avocado, and a touch of premium cheese. We season it with our proprietary blend of herbs and spices that enhances the natural flavors without overpowering them. A hint of truffle oil adds an elegant finish that distinguishes this spread from ordinary egg dishes.


Perfect on artisanal toast, bagels, or as part of a gourmet breakfast board, our Egg Spread comes in a convenient container that preserves its freshness and flavor. Whether you're hosting a sophisticated brunch gathering or treating yourself to a luxurious breakfast, this versatile spread will impress even the most discerning palates.


Ingredients: Free-range eggs, organic cream, chives, uncured bacon, avocado, aged cheddar, herbs, spices, and a touch of black truffle oil.
